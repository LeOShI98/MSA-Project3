ABOUT THIS SITE

The idea of this site is to encourge curiousity to explore, just like what a curious cat would do. Users can upload a picture and generate a little poem based on tags which might be assisiated with the picture.

In addition to gnerating to poem, there are also language selections so the users can have the freedom of choice and appritoate different cultures.

“The important thing is not to stop questioning. Curiosity has its own reason for existing.”

Albert Einstein
